Seeking medical attention or treatment

We will consider your claim whether you seek medical attention or not. However, if you have concerns about your pain or other symptoms and don't already have plans to see a physician, physiotherapist, chiropractor, or other qualified practitioner, we recommend you seek medical attention.

You can also seek physiotherapy right away. We will pay for an initial assessment from one of our contradicted physiotherapists even before we accept your claim. Lists of contracted physiotherapists are available on our website.

If you do seek medical attention or treatment, be sure to explain that your injury is work-related and give the provider your claim number.
